# GermanDict - Vocabulary & Conjugation Trainer

**GermanDict** (LearnWithHaxx) is a lightweight, fully offline Android application designed to help learners master German vocabulary, genders (articles), and verb conjugations. It combines modern UI elements with effective learning tools like a GitHub-style streak tracker and automated slideshows.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🎨 Visual Gender Learning
*   **Color-Coded Articles:** Automatically detects and colors German articles to help with memory retention:
    *   **der** (Masculine) - **Blue**
    *   **die** (Feminine) - **Red**
    *   **das** (Neuter) - **Green**
*   **Dedicated Nouns View:** Filter your vocabulary by gender with a single tap.

### 🏃 Smart Verb Conjugator
*   **Instant Conjugation:** Automatically generates present-tense forms (*ich, du, er/sie/es*, etc.) for any verb you add.
*   **Irregular Support:** Handles common irregular verbs, modal verbs, and separable prefixes.

### 📖 Interactive Learning
*   **Slideshow Mode:** Hands-free learning with automated slides and synchronized Text-to-Speech (TTS) in both German and English.
*   **Matching Game:** A fun way to test your knowledge by matching German words to their meanings.
*   **Pronunciation:** Integrated TTS allows you to hear the correct pronunciation of any word in your list.

### 📈 Progress Tracking
*   **Activity Streak:** A GitHub-style contribution grid that tracks your daily learning consistency.
*   **Daily Goals:** Set targets (e.g., 5 words a day) to maintain your streak.

### 💾 Backup & Sync
*   **CSV Import/Export:** Easily backup your entire vocabulary list or import words from a spreadsheet.
*   **Fully Offline:** No internet required. Your data stays on your device.
